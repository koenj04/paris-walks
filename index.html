Paris Walking Tours

How to use these walking tours:

iPhone Users:

  1. Tap the download button for your desired walk
  2. When prompted, choose your preferred mapping app (Maps.me, Google Maps, etc.)
  3. The route will open automatically in your chosen app

Android Users:

  1. Tap the download button for your desired walk
  2. Select "Open with" when prompted
  3. Choose your preferred mapping app from the list
